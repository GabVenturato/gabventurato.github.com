Hi, I’m Gabriele Venturato, a Machine Learning Research Engineer at [Segments.ai](https://segments.ai). I previously completed my PhD at [KU Leuven](https://www.kuleuven.be/kuleuven/) in the [DTAI Research Group](https://dtai.cs.kuleuven.be/), supervised by [Luc De Raedt](https://wms.cs.kuleuven.be/people/lucderaedt), and hold bachelor’s and master’s degrees in Computer Science from the [University of Udine](https://www.uniud.it).

I currently work on **3D object detection and machine learning systems**, with a background in neurosymbolic AI and reinforcement learning. My interests include building reliable learning-based systems by combining deep learning with structured reasoning and probabilistic methods.
